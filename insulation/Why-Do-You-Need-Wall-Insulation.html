Insulation is a great way to improve your home's energy efficiency, but it can also raise your home's resale value. Better Acoustics

Acoustic insulation helps control sound transmission between rooms. If a room is poorly insulated, it will be a noisy, unpleasant place, with echoes and other unwanted sounds bouncing around. This can also cause health problems, from hearing loss to depression. Acoustic insulation is an effective way of treating rooms that suffer from poor insulation.

Reduction of Heat Loss

Insulation is a key component to any energy efficiency project. The wall or ceiling is the largest surface in the house and the most likely to get cold spots. It is therefore critical to insulate the wall first, as it will require the least amount of insulation and will be the most expensive to insulate.
