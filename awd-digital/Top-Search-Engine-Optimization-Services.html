In today's competitive world, businesses are constantly trying to stand out from the competition, and one of the easiest ways to do so is through the use of SEO. SEO, or search engine optimization, is a key element of a website's success, as it allows a business to rank high in search results, allowing it to be found by potential customers online.

Top Search Engine Optimization Services

SEO service providers should include these key services in their offerings:

SEO audit

An SEO audit is the process of analyzing how well a web presence relates to SEO best practices. An SEO provider should take an in-depth look at where a customer currently stands with regards to current traffic and sales and analyze where the business excels and where it needs improvement. The audit will look at website features that are traditionally used to determine a website’s SERP ranking including broken links, title tags, and image alt text.

Competitor analysis

Competitive analysis involves researching the links and keywords of competitors, as well as their content, in order to reverse-engineer the most successful elements of these tactics into your own strategy. As a valued client we always make sure that our plans are on point and it's up to you to decide how you wish to proceed from there.

Custom strategy

A company’s SEO strategy should be tailored to their niche and specifications in order to get the most return on investment; this is a large priority for most customers.

Off-page optimization

Off-page optimization involves driving traffic to a website from other websites or resources on the Internet. This can be done through building links from relevant and authoritative sites. Links show search engines that your website conforms to certain Internet standards.

Monthly reports

An SEO provider should provide monthly performance reports that detail how well their implemented SEO strategy is working. This report may cover traffic generated through organic methods only, conversion rates, and rankings in search results.
